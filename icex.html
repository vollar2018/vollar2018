CEX

After ICEX is finished (block 30,240), the VDS Chain will automatically switch to CEX (Coin Exchange X). Here, Resonance Trade's Regular Phases begin,

where sustainable currency exchanges can be maintained. In CEX your currency is no longer multiplied, and the "X" now indicates limitless currency circulation. Participants may continue on performing Bitcoin to Vollar conversions whenever natural changes in the market make it worth it, as anytime the market price is higher than the Resonance Price this operation increases the legal tender value of the traded-in Bitcoin.

CEX Rules

After the VDS Chain begins, blocks 30,240-Infinity serve as the CEX Resonance Trade Phase. One Game will occur every 10,080 blocks throughout the entire, infinite lifetime of CEX. Any and all Bitcoin traded in CEX Phase game is distributed to the addresses belonging to the Fission Top 10 and Lucky 20 Light Master Node winners from the last game.

WARNING: THE ICEX PHASE RESONANCE ADDRESS IS ONLY TO BE USED DURING THE ICEX PHASE. If you send Bitcoin to the fixed ICEX address during the CEX phase, you will NOT receive any Vollar!!!!
