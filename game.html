The Game

The Game is one of many sophisticated designs within VDS which helps to balance and enrich the vitality of its ecology. It also brings a friendly user-experience and adds a flair of entertainment. In order to perfectly balance circulation within the financial network, as well as ensure its design upholds the principles of decentralization, VDS will directly distribute the Bitcoin raised during Resonance Trades to the winners of the Fission Promotion and Light Master Node Lottery games.

Game Phases

There are two distinct phase-types in the VDS Game, The Genesis Phase and Regular Phases. All the players' scores will be wiped clear at the end of every game, and the next game starts afresh.

Genesis Phase: Block 0 - 30,239

Regular Phases: Block 30,240-infinity, Every 10,080 blocks serve as one Regular Phase Phase (aka one Game, or one block-week).

Every VID user can participate in these Trust Stamp Network fission promotion games. It's like a real-life version of Ready Player One, but decentralized and based on blockchain!! The Top 10 winners split 50% of the Bitcoin rewards generated in Resonance Trade generated in the following 10,080 blocks (about one week) according to their scores in the fission-weighted scoring system.
